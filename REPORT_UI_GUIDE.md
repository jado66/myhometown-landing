## Quick Tips

### Column Ordering

1. **Select columns first**, then reorder them
2. Click â†‘ to move a column higher in the list
3. Click â†“ to move a column lower in the list
4. The order you see is the order in your exports

### Metadata Best Practices

- **Title**: Be specific about what the report shows
  - âœ… "Q4 2024 Volunteer Activity Report"
  - âŒ "Report"
- **Header**: Use for branding or department
  - âœ… "MyHometown Community Services"
  - âœ… "Volunteer Department - Annual Review"
- **Description**: Explain the report's purpose
  - âœ… "This report shows all volunteer activities from Oct-Dec 2024, including hours logged, activity types, and participant demographics."
  - âŒ "Data"

### Workflow

1. Select table and columns
2. **Reorder columns** to your preferred sequence
3. Add filters and sorting (optional)
4. Fill in **metadata** fields
5. Preview the data
6. **Save as template** (optional) - preserves order + metadata
7. Export to CSV or PDF
